Marriage is sacred. Your wedding is the culmination of your courtship, the beginning of deeper commitment and the celebration of your love and faith with family and friends. Your photographs are a bridge to your union and a legacy for future generations.

Susie and Sharlane draw on their extensive professional experience and education in photography to discreetly orchestrate the wedding day coverage. With an unobtrusive approach and intuition for seeing the loveliest potential in each interaction, they create timeless, elegant images unaffected by fads in photography. It is their deep held belief that wedding photojournalism should surpass mechanical recording and instead reflect the emotion and beauty of the day.

This collection of photographs reflects some of the moments Susie and Sharlane have been invited to witness and preserve. These images are born from real relationships and real weddings.
